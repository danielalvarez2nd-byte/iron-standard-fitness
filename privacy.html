Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

This policy explains how Iron Standard Fitness handles information collected through this website, the fitness quiz, purchases, and support requests. This is a practical storefront policy and should be reviewed by a qualified professional for your specific legal needs.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, quiz answers, product purchase details, support messages, and optional marketing consent. Fitness quiz responses may include goals, schedule, training experience, equipment access, and obstacles you choose to share.

How We Use Information

We use information to respond to quiz submissions, recommend a starting program, deliver purchased products, provide customer support, improve the website, and send marketing messages only when you have opted in.

Third-Party Services

Quiz submissions are handled through Tally. Product checkout, payment processing, digital delivery, receipts, and buyer records are handled through Gumroad. These services process information under their own policies and security practices.

Email And Marketing

Buyer emails are used for product delivery, receipts, and purchase support. Promotional email should only be sent to people who separately consented to marketing messages. You can request removal from marketing follow-up at any time.

Data Security

We rely on established third-party services for form handling, checkout, payment processing, and file delivery. No method of internet transmission or storage is perfectly secure, but these providers are used to avoid collecting payment information directly on this static site.

Your Choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you have submitted, subject to transaction, tax, fraud-prevention, and legal recordkeeping requirements handled by Gumroad or other service providers.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@ironstandardfitness.com.
